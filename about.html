About Me


BIO

I started to take interest in the front end developing about three years ago because I was very curious about how a slideshow effect is achieved in a website banner. I feel it was very interesting then I did some research on it. After a period of study, I learned that the animation effect was implemented by css and javascript and finally I implemented it by myself. After that, I start learning front-end programs and skills because I feel that is what I am passionate about. Last year, I made a important decision which I decided to switch my career path from being a hardware engineer to a front-end developer. It is not easy to jump from one field to another, but I am happy to make that decision because I can clearly see my future by getting on this path. Therefore, I set myself a few goals in my future career path. The first one, of course, I want to become a well experienced front-end developer, and after that, I eager to master the techniques of the back-end developing as well, and finally become a full-stack developer.
